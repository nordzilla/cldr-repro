This chart shows draft language groups based on data extracted from wikidata. The Status cell indicates the nature of the items in the adjacent Contained cell:

  • A 🌲 indicates that the contained languages are tree nodes (contain other languages or langauge groups), and will be listed further down in the chart in a Language Group cell.
  • A 🍃 indicates that the contained languages are leaf nodes (contain nothing).
  • A 🍂 before an item in a Contained cell indicates a leaf node that shouldn’t be — that is, its ISO 639 Scope is Collection.
